H.R.1 – One Big Beautiful Bill Act

This bill reduces taxes, reduces or increases spending for various federal programs, increases the statutory debt limit, and otherwise addresses agencies and programs throughout the federal government.

S.129 – No Tax on Tips Act

This bill establishes a new tax deduction of up to $25,000 for tips, subject to limitations. It also expands the business tax credit for payroll taxes paid on tips received in connection with beauty services.
